About Us

  • LaBallade is a concept cafe focusing primarily on Live Events. With multiple downtown locations, we at LaBallade want to provide you THE in-person cafe experience. Why order coffee online when you can taste delicious cuisine with Live music?

  • We at LaBallade want to empower the local community. LaBallade does not only hire local, but sources sustainable, organic, shade-grown beans for their coffee from local suppliers after careful back-checking, so our patrons can drink their coffee Guilt Free.

Our Story

We all have our bitter things in life. It's up to you how you taste it, sweeten it, and season it to be balanced. Our aim has always been to provide THE cafe experience to people through our events. But the fun shouldn't come at a cost to the environment. We support our local suppliers and only use organically source coffee beans. Now you can enjoy your coffe, Guilt Free.
